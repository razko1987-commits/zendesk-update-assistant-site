Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

Yes. All ticket data remains within your Zendesk instance. We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal data outside of Zendesk. No tracking, analytics, or third-party services are used.

Will this app interfere with my Zendesk workflows?

No. The app only stages changes as drafts. It never auto-submits or alters your existing Zendesk workflows. You maintain full control over when and how updates are submitted.

Can I customize the update templates?

Yes, with the Standard plan you can create and manage custom templates. The Basic plan includes one general-purpose template.

What happens if I need help?

Visit our support page to submit questions, bug reports, or feature requests. Standard plan users receive priority support.

When will the Pro plan be available?

The Pro plan is currently in development. Please visit our support page to express interest and we'll notify you when it becomes available.
