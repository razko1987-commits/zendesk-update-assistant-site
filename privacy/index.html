Privacy Policy

Your data stays in your Zendesk instance. Internal Note Helper does not collect, store, or transmit personal data outside of Zendesk.

Data Handling

All ticket data remains within your Zendesk instance. We do not access, store, or process any data outside of the Zendesk platform where the app is installed.

No Tracking or Analytics

Internal Note Helper does not use any tracking, analytics, or third-party services. We do not collect usage statistics, user behavior data, or any other information about how you use the app.

Data Security

Since all data processing occurs within your Zendesk instance, your existing Zendesk security measures and data protection policies apply. We do not have access to your data and cannot view, modify, or export any information from your Zendesk account.

Third-Party Services

Internal Note Helper does not integrate with any third-party services or external APIs. All functionality is self-contained within the Zendesk platform.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please visit our support page.
